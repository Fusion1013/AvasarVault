# Overview
A company specialized in advanced material fabrication and enchantment.
# History
The company originally formed as a small workshop that produced stone tiles that provide different effects. These could for example be embedded into the floor of someone's home, or in a town square.

The [[Sigilwright Conclave]] got its first major contract when it was hired by the [[Ohkyr Dominion Overview|Dominion]] to start construction on a vast highway spanning the width of the nation. This catapulted the company from a small, local organization to a nation-spanning major organization. They are now one of the largest private companies in the nation, and provide work for a large number of people in most major cities.

# Establishments
The company owns a majority of industrial land in [[Halzarin]], which is where their main fabrication plant is located. They also operate most of the port of the city, which they have expanded significantly since they moved operations there. The company also has multiple rare-mineral extraction mines around the nation, as well as in other nations.
# Production
## Enchanted Tiles
The tiles that make up the Highroads contain a type of volcanic stone that can only be found in the [[Akhalun Isles]]. The stone is crushed into a fine powder and mixed with red clay dust, which is then pressed into moulds. After the tiles have dried, they are brought into large kilns and heated to high temperatures. Charcoal is used to heat the kilns and forges in the city. The charcoal is primarily sourced from the forest just north of [[Halzarin]].

After the base tiles have been made the process of enchanting them can begin. The actual process of enchanting the tiles is a highly guarded secret, only known by the [[Sigilwright Conclave]].
