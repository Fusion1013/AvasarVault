# Overview
There are numerous islands dotted around the oceans that are technically not part of any nation.
# Tanüera
Tanüera is an island group north of [[Ohkyr Dominion Overview|Ohkyr]]. It consists of five main islands, with a bunch of smaller ones on the outskirts. The entire island group is surrounded by a large coral reef, with smaller islands of sharp rocks dotted between. Getting in to the island with a ship is extremely difficult.

The islands are mostly covered in jungle. The main island has a volcano in the middle, with jungle surrounding it. The second-largest island also has a mountain on it, but it is not volcanic.
